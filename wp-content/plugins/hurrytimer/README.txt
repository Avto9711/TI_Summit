== Description ==

[HurryTimer](https://hurrytimer.com/) allows you to create urgency and scarcity that drives clicks, increase sales, boost conversions, and highlights upcoming events or deadlines. 

[Check out HurryTimer PRO!](https://hurrytimer.com/)

## LITE FEATURES

### ▶ Evergreen Countdown timer (countdown timer per visitor)

Give each visitor their own unique countdown timer regardless of their local timezone and the moment they visited your site.

### ▶ Regular Countdown timer (Countdown to date)

Run campaign that expires after a specific date. No matter who visits your site the countdown timer is the same.

### ▶ Integrated with WooCommerce

Easily display a countdown timer on the product page to drive more "add to cart" button clicks.

### ▶ Expiry actions

Take action when campaign expires:

- Hide countdown timer.
- Redirect to URL.
- Display a message.
- Change stock status.
- Hide "Add to cart" button.

### ▶ Customize to fit your site design

Visually customize the countdown timer style to fit your site's design.

### ▶ Display Anywhere

Use shortcode to display the countdown timer anywhere on your site, including: Classic editor, Block editor (Gutenberg), Widgets, Pages builders, etc. 

### ▶ Multiple instances on the same page

Display multiple instances of the same countdown timer on the same page.

### ▶ And more:
- Auto-Reset expired campaign
- Custom timer labels: edit days, hours, minutes, seconds labels.
- Call-to-Action button.
- Compatible with all page builders out-of-the-box.

### Usage

1. Visit "Hurrytimer > Add New Timer".
2. Choose between evergreen and regular mode.
3. Enter a period for "Evergreen" mode, or select a date and time for "Regular" mode.
4. Click on "Publish".
5. Copy shortCode and paste it into your post, page, or text widget content. You can also go to "WooCommerce" tab to integrate the countdown timer directly within a product page.

== Installation ==

### From within WordPress

1. Visit _Plugins > Add New_.
2. Search for _Hurrytimer_.
3. Install the plugin.
4. Activate the plugin.

### Manually

1. Upload the _hurrytimer_ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the Hurrytimer plugin through the _Plugins_ menu in WordPress.
